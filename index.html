ViewRoom(VR) Meetings

The Problem.

At the University of the West Indies, an increasing number of courses are being streamed to and from other campuses. As the number of persons depending on these streaming services increases, the quality of the lectures being streamed to these campuses becomes of greater concern. In particular maintaining student engagement in these virtual classrooms has been an issue.
​
Remote students are often at a disadvantage in the classroom and are unable to fully immerse in exercises being done in class. Many presentations and in class activities cannot be effectively conveyed through the limited streaming options offered natively by the current streaming option, Zoom. This is especially evident in classes where the lecturer regularly needs to do more than just present a powerpoint presentation.

Presenting VR Meetings.
ViewRoom Meetings is a web based add-on to Zoom which facilitates a more immersive and engaging interaction with students in streamed classes. It offers features which allow both students and lecturers to get more out of their virtual classroom experience.

Features.

Integrates with Zoom

Our application is easy to use and we integrate with the popular video conferencing software Zoom.

Object Tracking

Users can select persons or objects in the 360 video stream that they would like to watch in more detail.
This creates a view in Zoom that focuses on only that object and follows the object wherever it moves within the 360 video stream.

360 Camera  

Our application integrates with a 360 camera to give you a more immersive experience in your next class.

Select your View

Users can select a section of the room in the 360 video stream that they would like to watch in more detail.
This creates a view in Zoom that focuses on only that section of the original 360 video stream.

Coming Soon.

Integration with VR Headsets

Our application will allow users to use Virtual Reality headsets to enhance even more, the quality of the classroom experience.
​You will be able to immerse yourself in the room completely with a virtual 360 view of the classroom.

Integration with more Software

Our application will integrate with other video conferencing software such as Google Hangouts.
